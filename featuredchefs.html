Featured Chefs

These chefs are just the tip of the iceberg.

Don't see your favorite chef or one you would like to know more about?

Reach out via the Contact Me section.

Anne Burrell

Occupation: TV personality, "Worst Cooks in America"

Her wheelhouse: Italian, comfort food

Likes: Pasta, sparkles

Dislikes: Cooks that don't listen to her instructions

Why her?: She holds her own while cooking great food in a male dominated industry. And she has cool hair.

Jose Andres

Occupation: Chef/Owner: ThinkFoodGroup

Her wheelhouse: Spanish cuisine, small plates

Likes: Washington Wizards, golf

Dislikes: Gordon Ramsey

Why him?: He is passionate about American cultures and it shows in his food. In addition to his spanish heritage, he draws inspiration for some of his dishes from colonial times.

Bobby Flay

Occupation: TV personality, Chef/Owner: Mesa Grill, Gato, Bobby's Burger Palace

His wheelhouse: Southwestern flavors

Likes: Bourbon, ice-cream

Dislikes: Not cooking with peppers

Why him?: He is always full of ideas; he has been behind many of his successful shows. He is also a New Yorker with quite a relatable tale about his journey into the restaurant business.

Carla Hall

Occupation: TV personality, "The Chew" Chef/Owner: Carla Hall's Southern Kitchen (coming 2016)

Her wheelhouse: Baking, Southern cuisine

Likes: Vegetables, her catachphrase "Hootie Hoo"

Dislikes: Meat heavy dishes

Why her?: If her affinity for baking doesn't win your heart, then watch this montage from her "Top Chef" season 5 days.

Martha Stewart

Occupation: Founder, Marta Stewart Omnimedia

Her wheelhouse: Home decor, extravagance

Likes: Inported goods

Dislikes: Terrible presentation

Why her?: She is the go-to for elegant dinner parties. Her expensive taste might set you back a paycheck or two, but boy your dinner party will be fancy. Plus she knows how to make homemade clay.

Tom Colicchio

Occupation: Head Judge, "Top Chef" Chef/Owner: Craft, Colicchio & Sons

His wheelhouse: American cuisine

Likes: Great food, fishing

Dislikes: Very spicy food; slacker Top Chef contestants

Why him?: He is a serious advocate on food policies; he emphasizes the importance of SNAP, farming, and GMO labeling.

Alton Brown

Occupation: TV personality, "Cutthroat Kitchen"

His wheelhouse: perfecting execution of dishes

Likes: Causing misery to "Cutthroat Kitchen" contestants

Dislikes: Single-purpose kitchen utensils

Why him?: He knows about food. A LOT. This was especially clear during his days as commentator on "Iron Chef America", no matter the dishes, he had an answer or fun fact at the ready. Plus he has a dark sense of humor for people into that sort of thing.

Andrew Zimmern

Occupation: Culinary Traveler, "Bizarre Foods"

His wheelhouse: Cuisines of the world

Likes: Head cheese, bull testicles

Dislikes: Walnuts, oatmeal, Spam

Why him?: His enthuasism for culinary exploration through food is infectious. Some people see him only as the guy that eats weird food, however, his adventures around the world has afforded him an expansive repertoire of flavor combinations.
