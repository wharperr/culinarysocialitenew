Top Chef by Seasons

As Seen on Bravo.

Season 1 - San Francisco

  • Stephen Asprinio

    thought that he could change the title of the show from "Top Chef" to "Top Sommelier". Spent most of his time talking about wine pairings and why he is so awesome.

    Episodes 1-8

  • Restaurant Wars

    was born. This elimination would go on to be the most iconic challenge for the show. Most chefestants and diehard TC fans look forward to this episode every season.

    Episode 7

  • All of the chefs...

    ...thought that using box cake mixes would be a good idea for a WEDDING challenge. Tom chews them out aplenty for this rookie decision.

    Episode 8

  • Harold Dieterle

    was crowned the first Top Chef

    Epsiode 12


Season 2 - Los Angeles

  • Marcel Vigneron

    becomes the 1st Top Chef villian. Not sure whether his culinary agility or his ego irked his fellow contestants more.

    All Season

  • Michael Midgley

    thought it would be okay to stick a cheeto in a chocolate candy bar mound and called it an amuse bouche. In Friday Junk Night, this might be okay, however, in a high-stakes food competition, slackitude is unacceptable.

    Episode 4

  • Cliff

    was the 1st (and only) contestant to be eliminated from the competiton for being physically aggressive with Marcel. This foolish incident would have a long-term impact on his career. Perhaps his parents did not teach him to keep his hands to himself.

    Episode 11

  • Top Chef went abroad (kind of)...

    ...to Hawaii where the contestants faced traditional Hawaiian ingredients and dishes.

    Episodes 12 & 13


Season 3 - Miami

  • Bertolli

    shows everyone the complexity of packaging frozen dinners to preserve both shelf-life and taste. I will never look at a Hungry Man dinner the same...

    Episode 7

  • Casey

    made us cry when she took forever to dice onions in the Quickfire challenge. Also, Hung proves just how swift he is in the kitchen when he butchers down four (4) whole chickens.

    Episode 10

  • CJ

    showed us exactly why we despise airplane food. I would take stale pretzels over his broccolini anyday.

    Episode 12

  • Hung Huynh

    is crowned Top Chef, making him the 1st Asian (Vietnamese) chef to win the title.

    Episode 15


Season 4 - Chicago

  • Everyone

    flips out on each other. Spike on Antonia, Dale on Lisa, and Jen-- on the furniture. There are moments like these that demonstrate the brillance and necessity of reality television.

    Episode 5

  • The Second City

    allows an audience of people who enjoy comedic improvisation to pick ingredients for the upcoming Elimination challege. Missteps abound!

    Episode 10

  • Spike

    finally spikes the nerve of the worng person-- the guest judge Rick Tramonto. Telling the man that the reason he hated your dish started with frozen scallops in his restaurant kitchen probably was not a good idea. Don't worry folks, Spike is doing very well for himself with his restaurant empire

    Episode 12

  • Stephanie Izard

    becomes the 1st woman to win the Top Chef title. Her flavors and versatility defeat the favorite to win Richard Blais, who threw in the towel in the end. (How exactly did Lisa get to be a runner-up?)

    Episode 14


Season 5 - New York

  • The Foo Fighters

    requested a Thanksgiving dinner for themselves and 60 members of their entourage, many being vegetarians. That is a really tall order. (I was preparing Thanksgiving dinner that night and found my Thankgiving dinner of 15 to pale in comparison.

    Episode 3

  • Former Bears players

    serve as guest judges on a tailgating challenge. Ryan Scott decides that bread salad (yuck!) and poached pears are suitable for a football game. So, no buffalo wings? *sigh*

    Episode 10

  • Toby Young

    holds nothing back with the contestants or his fellow judges. "The U.N. weapons inspectors were looking in the wrong place in the run up to the Iraq war. Because I have found the weapons of mass destruction, and they are in this bowl." [About Radhika's seafood bisque] Zing!

    All season

  • Carla Hall

    decides to listen to onion lady Casey during her final challenge. This mistep would cost her the Top Chef title. Hosea Rosenberg wins by default. Yes the guy that kissed Leah Cohen while they were both in relationships.

    Episode 14


Season 6 - Las Vegas

  • Michael Voltaggio

    wins Top Chef in the 1st episode. Psyche! He doesn't but the judges show their preference for him and his brother Bryan Voltaggio from the start.

    Episode 1

  • Money

    was a big player in this Las Vegas season. In addition to the $125,000 cash prize, Quickfires and Elimination challenges winners recieved prizes ranging from $10,000-$30,000. The Top Chef winner also recieved a Toyota Prius (Disclaimer: I am not being paid to mention Toyota).

    Episodes 1, 2, 5, 7, 9 & 13

  • Top Chef

    premieres its line of frozen foods. Because the cookbooks, merchandise, and endorsements from external companies does not generate enough revenue...

    Episode 10

  • Bocuse d'Or

    comes to Top Chef. This is a highly competitive international competiton in which the U.S. almost never places. Perfection does not even accurately describe the standard dishes are held to in this arena. Kevin Gillespie rightfully declines following through with a spot on the U.S. team.

    Episode 14


Season 7 - Washington D.C.

  • The Lost Season

    This was a pretty blah season. I don't remember most of the episodes.

  • Kevin Sbraga

    becomes the next Top Chef. is the 1st African-American to be crowned Top Chef.

    Episode 14


Season 8 - New York City

  • Top Chef favorites

    return for an all-star season. Drama and hi-jinxs ensue.

  • Jennifer Caroll

    proves she is a very sore loser. Curse words fly!

    Episode 2

  • Cookie Monster

    and Elmo and Telly from "Sesame Street" pop in for some cookies!

    Episode 10

  • Richard Blais

    gets his groove back and takes the title of Top Chef. All over America, cheers erupted.

    Episode 14


Season 9 - Texas

  • Last Chance Kitchen

    premieres online following each episode. The comapnion show gives eliminated chefs the opportunity to compete to win another chance in the competition.

  • Mean Girls

    prove that bitterness and spite doesn't win the competiton or fans.

  • Nyesha Arrington

    pays the price for her teammates blunder. Somewhere in there is a lesson about teamwork...

    Episode 10

  • Paul Qui

    is the 1st Filipino chef awarded the Top Chef title. If you are ever in Texas, check out his restaurants.

    Episode 17
